Exponential Prelude for piano trio and Live Electronics, 8’


yongwoo lee · Exponential Prelude For Trio And Live Electronics Midi Generated Sound

Exponential Prelude for piano trio and live electronics is a piece conceived from the properties of time found in gradual and curving functions that appear in exponential functions. The piece that starts slow does not speed up linearly, but curves like an exponential function. The viola and cello build up like artificial overtones from the low notes of the piano, and are subdivided rhythmically. The electronic sounds that respond to the sound of each instrument in the trio take time and slope as parameters having various graph shapes, and the ratio of various points is obtained according to the nums dividing the octave into halves in the exponential function. It gets the rhythm and pitch from the graph and adds it to the actual live sound source, dealing with the subtle sounds.

These pitches create an artificial harmonics series through specific points derived from parameters, and the electronic sounds that gradually appear in proportion to the slope of the exponential function, such as the overtone gradually appearing from the base, as in the meaning of the overtone. Gradually, it reacts immediately to the sound of live instruments without delay, and accordingly, artificial harmonic sequences are combined with the sound of real instruments to become a single timbre. The rhythm gradually becomes denser and the tempo faster, as if reminiscent of the exponential function becoming denser. And the relatively consonant parts of the piece that appear in the middle of the composition of the piece evoke these artificial harmonics as a natural harmonic sequence. It means to be, and these characteristics make it possible to recall the state of mathematical alignment and misalignment.

It is rather more natural for living things to be curved, like the change in the cycle of time when a creature's running gradually stops, rather than a symmetrical and straight line. This piece was made that way.
